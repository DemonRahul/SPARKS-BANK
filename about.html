    All About GRIP ?

    The Graduate Rotational Internship Program is a unique offer for students and recent graduates to experience and join The Sparks Foundation.
    In addition to skills-specific tasks, we encourage interns to build a credible professional profile.

    Inspiring

    To Inspire, motivate and encourage students to learn, create and help build a better society.

    Innovating

    To teach new ways of thinking, to innovate and solve the problems on their own.

    Integrating

    To let the students integrate, help each other, learn from each other and do well together.
